This document introduces **Gimhae**, a historic city in South Korea.  
It is designed for travelers and learners who want to explore Korean history and culture.

## About Gimhae

Gimhae is located in the southeastern part of South Korea, near Busan.  
It is known for its rich history, including connections to the **Gaya Kingdom**, one of Korea's ancient kingdoms.

## Famous Places

- **Gimhae National Museum (김해국립박물관)**: A museum showcasing artifacts from the Gaya Kingdom and Korean history.
- **Bonghwangdae Observatory (봉황대 전망대)**: Offers panoramic views of Gimhae and the surrounding countryside.
- **Daedong Sports Park (대동체육공원)**: A recreational area with walking trails and sports facilities.
- **Gaya Theme Park (가야테마파크)**: A cultural park highlighting the heritage and traditions of the Gaya Kingdom.

## Food to Try

- **Gimhae Galbi (김해갈비)**: Grilled marinated ribs, a local specialty.
- **Hwajeon (화전)**: Sweet flower pancakes, often enjoyed during festivals.
- **Rice Cakes (떡)**: Various types of traditional Korean rice cakes available at local markets.

> Tip: Visit historical sites and local markets to experience the traditional culture of Gimhae.
