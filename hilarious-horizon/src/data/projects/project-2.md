This document teaches you **basic Korean numbers**.  
It is designed for learners who want to study Korean.

- **하나 (Hana)**: One
- **둘 (Dul)**: Two
- **셋 (Set)**: Three
- **넷 (Net)**: Four
- **다섯 (Daseot)**: Five

> Tip: Practice counting objects around you in Korean to remember the numbers.
