This document teaches you **basic Korean greetings**.  
It is designed for learners who want to study Korean.

- **안녕하세요 (Annyeonghaseyo)**: Hello (formal greeting)
- **안녕 (Annyeong)**: Hi / Bye (informal greeting)
- **감사합니다 (Gamsahamnida)**: Thank you (formal)
- **고마워 (Gomawo)**: Thanks (informal)

> Tip: Try to memorize one expression every day and use it in real conversations.
